# Community code of conduct

We want participation in `oss-ready` to be safe, constructive, and welcoming.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, experience levels, and viewpoints.
- Give specific, actionable feedback and assume good intent.
- Keep technical disagreements focused on the work.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing another person's information.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, deliberate
disruption, and publishing private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through GitHub's repository contact options.
Maintainers may edit or remove content, issue warnings, temporarily restrict
participation, or permanently ban participants when necessary. Reports will be
handled as confidentially as practical, and retaliation is prohibited.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone publicly represents the
project.
